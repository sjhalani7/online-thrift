Our Story

Founded by two friends in 2017, The Wardrobe Project started as a weekend flea market booth and has grown into Oakland's go-to spot for nostalgia lovers. We believe every piece tells a story, and we're passionate about connecting our community with sustainable fashion and one-of-a-kind finds. Come explore and see what vintage treasures you'll uncover!
